## Full description (4000 chars max)
AuraSound turns your phone into a personal ambient sound studio. Layer up to four
high-quality sounds, balance each one with its own volume slider, and build the exact
atmosphere you need to focus, relax, or drift off to sleep.

Everything plays fully offline — no account, no sign-in, no streaming. Just open the
app and press play.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Build your own mix
  Combine up to four sounds at once and fine-tune the volume of each channel
  independently. A master control adjusts the whole blend and lets you clear the mix
  in one tap.

• A library of calming sounds
  Choose from four curated categories:
  – Focus: white noise, pink noise, and brown noise
  – Nature: light & heavy rain, distant thunder, ocean waves, forest birds, river stream
  – Ambient: campfire crackle, soft wind, coffee shop hum, train on tracks, electric fan
  – Sleep: Tibetan singing bowl, deep space drone, night crickets, slow heartbeat

• Save your favorite presets
  Found a blend you love? Save it with a custom name and reload it instantly any time —
  "Cozy Cabin," "Deep Focus," "Ocean Sleep," whatever fits your mood.

• Sleep timer with smooth fade-out
  Set a timer (15 to 90 minutes) and let AuraSound gently fade the volume down over the
  final minutes so the sound eases away instead of stopping abruptly.

• A calm, distraction-free interface
  A premium midnight theme with a soft pulsing visualizer keeps the focus on your sound,
  not the screen. Built for phones and tablets alike with an adaptive layout.

WHY AURASOUND

Whether you're studying, working, meditating, calming a restless mind, masking a noisy
environment, or settling in for the night, AuraSound gives you full control over your
sound environment — simple, beautiful, and completely offline.

Press play and find your calm.
